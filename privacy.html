Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Merxio. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data Collection

Offline-First: Merxio is designed as an offline-first application. All your business data, including inventory, sales, and customer information, is stored locally on your device.

We do not collect or store your business data on our servers. Your data stays with you.

3. Permissions

Our app may request the following permissions:

  • Camera: Used solely for barcode scanning to process sales and manage inventory.
  • Storage: Used to store the app database and export reports (CSV).

4. Third-Party Services

Merxio does not share your data with third-party services. Since the data remains on your device, we have no access to it.

5. Security

We recommend using device-level security (passcodes, biometrics) to protect the data stored in Merxio on your phone.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at restsofts@gmail.com.
